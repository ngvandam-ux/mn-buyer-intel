Phone Call Scam Alert: We’re aware of reported phone calls that claim to be from the state of Minnesota asking state vendors to update banking information. If you get a phone call, text message or email asking you to do that, hang up or delete the message from your device. Do not reply or click on any link. We will never send unsolicited messages asking for banking information.

Supplier Portal

Click here to be redirected to the Supplier Portal to register as a Bidder or Supplier for the State of Minnesota.

Click here to be redirected to a page for information on State of Minnesota contracts expiring within the next seven (7) months.
